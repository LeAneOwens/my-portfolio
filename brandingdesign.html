Adopt a Familiar Cat Rescue

This is a fictional cat rescue created to showcase my design abilities. I wanted something whimsical, but professional in feel for this example. Most of my animals I have ever had have been rescues. If I were to be tasked with creating any sort of fictional business, I think any sort of animal rescue is only natural for me. A Halloween-adjacent theme is also second nature, but rest assure I can adapt to any theme that's requested of me.
