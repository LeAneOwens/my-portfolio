Cat on the Moon

The inspiration of this animation a white cat I raised from an orphaned newborn kitten alongside his sisters. I caught him on the table one day just staring at a coffee cup when he was old enough to climb. I recognized the expression on his face that fortold mischief, but I couldn't react in time before he took his paw and knocked the cup over to the floor with all the intrigue of a scientist performing an experiment. He was the troublemaker of the litter.
